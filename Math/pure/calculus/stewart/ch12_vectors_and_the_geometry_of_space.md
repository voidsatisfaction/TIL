  - Sphere
    - 중심 `C(h,k,l)`, 반지름 r
      - `(x-h)^2 + (y-k)^2 + (z-l)^2 = r^2`
    - e.g)
      - `1 ≤ x^2+y^2+z^2 ≤ 4, z≤0`
