## 5.1 Area Between Curves

- Area Between Curves
  - `f,g는 연속`
    - `A = lim(n->∞)(sigma_{i=1}^{n}(|f(xi*) - g(xi*)|Δx))`
    - `= int_a^b|f(xi*) - g(xi*)|dx`
